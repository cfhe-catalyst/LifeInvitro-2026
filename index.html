Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions Answered

Who are the courses suitable for?
Our courses are designed for practising embryologists, IVF lab technicians, andrology staff, clinicians, paramedical personnel, and new entrants with a background in life sciences. Each course page specifies recommended prerequisites.
Where are the courses held?
All programs are conducted at the LifeInvitro Academy facility located at IIT Hyderabad, Sangareddy, Telangana. The campus provides a fully equipped ART training laboratory with industry-standard instruments.
What accommodation is available?
On-campus accommodation is available at the IIT Hyderabad guest house, subject to availability. Several hotels and rental options are also available near the campus for participants travelling from other cities.
Are certificates recognized in the industry?
Yes. Participants receive a Certificate of Completion issued under the LifeInvitro Academy and CfHE, IIT Hyderabad. These certificates are recognized by fertility clinics and IVF units across India as evidence of structured, hands-on clinical training.
What is the batch size for each course?
Batches are intentionally kept small to ensure individualized attention and adequate time on equipment. Exact capacities vary per course. We recommend registering early, as seats fill quickly — particularly for ICSI and Embryo Biopsy.
Can I attend multiple courses in one visit?
Yes, where dates allow. For example, the START course (Aug 29–30) is immediately followed by the Fundamentals of Clinical ART Laboratory (Sep 1–10), making it an ideal two-week foundational immersion for new entrants.
How do I register or enquire about fees?
You can register through the LifeInvitro Academy website at cfhe.org.in/lifeinvitro-academy, email the academy directly, or send a WhatsApp message using the inquiry button on this page. Our team will respond within one working day.
What are MICROEMBART and ACE 2026?
MICROEMBART (Jun 6–7) and ACE (Aug 7–9) are special annual ART events hosted by LifeInvitro Academy bringing together practitioners, trainers, and industry partners for demonstrations, lectures, and networking. Attendance is open to professionals in the ART field.
